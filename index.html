AGATHE MESLEM

Graphic and Digital Design Student

École supérieure d'art et de design d'Amiens

Driven by a deep fascination with visual storytelling, I am a dedicated graphic and digital design student passionate about how design can shape experiences and create meaningful connections. With a strong foundation in design software, typography, and layout techniques, I strive to craft visuals that not only capture attention but also evoke thought and emotion.

My design approach is influenced by diverse interests, including chess, poetry, surrealism, and marine life—elements that often bring a blend of precision, whimsy, and depth to my work. Additionally, my interest in drag culture has taught me to embrace boldness and fluidity, allowing for a playful yet intentional style in the narratives I create.

I am eager to continue learning and contributing my unique perspective to the design field, pushing the boundaries of creativity in typography, editorial layout, and immersive digital projects.
